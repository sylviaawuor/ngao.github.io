Motisha na Ngao Credit

Motisha from Ngao Credit is the service that lets you Choose, Relax and Drive. With Motisha, you select the car you want to buy, pay 50% of the market value as deposit and sit back as we handle your vehicle’s documentation, processing, and importation. Our seamless end to end solution will deliver your dream car in just 60 days! When you import with Motisha, you get flexible repayment periods of up to 2 years with Interests as low as 3.5%, all in three easy steps.
